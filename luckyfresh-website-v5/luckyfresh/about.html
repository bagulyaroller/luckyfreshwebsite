Our Roots

Rooted in the Heartland of India's Wheat Belt

In 2020, the Bagulya family established Bagulya Roller Flour Mill (BRFM) in Ashoknagar, Madhya Pradesh, under the beloved brand Jain Sahab Ka Lucky Fresh. The goal was simple yet ambitious: deliver flour that was fresher, purer, and more nutritious than anything available in the region.

Today, Lucky Fresh serves thousands of retailers, bakeries, hotels, and households across Madhya Pradesh and neighbouring states with a production capacity of 350 MT (3500 Quintal) per day.

The founding principle remains unchanged: Purity in Every Grain.

Our Mission

To deliver the highest quality flour products to every household and business — maintaining strict standards of hygiene, nutrition, and taste while building a sustainable business that benefits our farmers, employees, and distribution partners.

To become the most trusted flour brand across Central India. We aim to expand our product portfolio, increase milling capacity, and reach 25+ states through a strong network of partners.
